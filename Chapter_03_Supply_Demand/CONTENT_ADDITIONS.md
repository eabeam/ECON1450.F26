# New Content Added to Presentation

## GIFs and Interactive Demo Now Embedded

### 1. Movement vs. Shift Animation
**Location:** After "Common Mistakes: Movement vs. Shift" slide
**File:** `images/movement_demand.gif`
**Shows:** Visual distinction between movement along curve vs. shift of entire curve
**Purpose:** Reinforces the most common conceptual mistake students make

### 2. Shortage Adjustment Animation
**Location:** After "Shortage: When Price is Too Low" slide
**File:** `images/shortage_adjustment.gif`
**Shows:** Dynamic adjustment from shortage back to equilibrium
**Purpose:** Demonstrates market self-correction mechanism

### 3. Demand Increase Animation
**Location:** After "When Demand Increases" slide
**File:** `images/demand_increase.gif`
**Shows:** Demand curve shifting right and new equilibrium forming
**Purpose:** Visualizes comparative statics analysis

### 4. Supply Decrease Animation
**Location:** After "When Supply Decreases" slide
**File:** `images/supply_decrease.gif`
**Shows:** Supply curve shifting left and new equilibrium forming
**Purpose:** Completes the four basic shift scenarios

### 5. Interactive Plotly Demo (NEW!)
**Location:** After "Simultaneous Shifts: Handle with Care" slide
**File:** `interactive_simultaneous_shifts_v2.html`
**Features:**
- Sliders to shift supply curve (-3 to +3)
- Sliders to shift demand curve (-3 to +3)
- Real-time graph updates
- Market effects panel showing:
  - Price change (amount and direction)
  - Quantity change (amount and direction)
  - Effect decomposition
**Styled as:** Teal button with emoji and shadow
**Opens in:** New browser tab for full interactivity

**Total GIFs:** 4 embedded animations throughout presentation
